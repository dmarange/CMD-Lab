# Setting Up Your Alliance Canada Account (Fir + SFU)

To use the Digital Research Alliance of Canada (Alliance Canada) HPC systems, you need an active user account linked to SFU and your supervisor's research group. The steps below cover registration and access for the Fir system.

Step 1: Register for an account
- Go to https://ccdb.alliancecan.ca/security/login and click Register. Use your SFU email (e.g abc123@sfu.ca) and provide accurate supervisor details so your account is linked to the correct research group.
- Supervisor Details: eus-881-02 , Mohammadreza Karamad: Faculty, Chemistry, Simon Fraser University

Step 2: Request system access (Fir)
- Once your account is created, request access to the Fir system here: https://ccdb.alliancecan.ca/me/access_systems (also found under the Resources menu). Some systems require accepting usage agreements on this page.

Step 3: Check the Alliancecan wiki for help if needed: https://docs.alliancecan.ca/wiki/Getting_started

Important notes:
For other systems, you must manually request access.

Verify your access status: green checkmark = enabled.
