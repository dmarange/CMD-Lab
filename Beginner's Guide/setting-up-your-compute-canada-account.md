# Setting Up Your Compute Canada Account

To use Compute Canada's high-performance computing (HPC) systems, you need an active user account. These are the new set up and login instructions given recently updates to the clusters.

Step 1: Register for an account
- Go to https://ccdb.alliancecan.ca/security/login and click Register. Provide accurate information-especially your supervisor's details-to ensure your account is correctly linked to your research group.

Step 2: Request system access
- Once your account is created, you must request access to specific HPC and cloud systems: https://ccdb.alliancecan.ca/me/access_systems (also found under the Resources menu). Some systems also require accepting usage agreements on this page.

Important notes:
For other systems, you must manually request access.

Verify your access status: green checkmark = enabled.

These steps ensure you can log in and start using Compute Canada resources for your calculations.
